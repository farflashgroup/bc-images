UHN recruits senior NIH investigator, more than 70 global scientists

Toronto's University Health Network is staging Canada Leads to bring in 100 early- to mid-career scientists from abroad, with more than 70 recruits already secured. The UHN Foundation and Princess Margaret Cancer Foundation have committed $63.8 million to the effort.
