Canada Groceries and Essentials Benefit

Canada Groceries and Essentials Benefit takes effect

A one-time top-up landed June 5. Enriched quarterly payments start July 3.

Quick explainer

Ottawa renamed the GST/HST credit and raised payment amounts. Eligibility and income tests are unchanged.

Who qualifies

12M

Low- and modest-income Canadians based on tax filing information. Not all households apply.

Happening now

50%

One-time top-up on your 2025-26 GST credit. Based on your 2024 tax return.

Happening on July 3

+25%

GST credit renamed. Quarterly payments rise 25% for five years.

2026 maximum

$950 - $1,890

Single person with top-up in 2026: up to $950. Family of four with top-up: up to $1,890. Ongoing annual max (2026-27): $679 single, $890 couple, $234 per child under 19.
