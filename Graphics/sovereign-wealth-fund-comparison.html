Initial Size

$500B+

Target within 10 years; top-5 globally by 2050.

Key Difference
Funding Source

Federal contribution (from federal deficit) plus a retail product for Canadians. Possible monetization of federal assets later.

Restored pre-2015 federal spending levels (75% to fund), matched provincial royalties, asset sales, windfall taxes.

Where Invested

Domestic — energy, critical minerals, agriculture, and infrastructure projects.

Primarily global — 75% passive in world equity & bonds; domestic capped at 10%.

Governance

Arm's-length federal Crown corporation. Mandate to be finalized by transition office.

Federal-provincial-territorial-Indigenous board. CPPIB manages active portion for first 5 years.

Citizen Benefit

Voluntary retail investment product — Canadians earn returns on their own contributions.

Universal Canada Dividend from year 6 — 20% of net earnings paid annually to every citizen.

Safeguards

To be defined in the Spring Economic Update 2026 and through public consultation.

Sovereign Wealth Fund Act: principal protection, audits, 0.50% management-fee cap.

Sources: Office of the Prime Minister of Canada (Apr 27, 2026); Build Canada — Build Canada's Wealth Fund Memo (Apr 2025). Details of the Canada Strong Fund's governance, mandate, and retail product are still pending consultation.
