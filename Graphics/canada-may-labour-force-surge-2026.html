What it means

Summer hiring is real, but part-time fell. Youth full-time rose 99,000 while national part-time work dropped 66,000.

World Cup prep likely added to the uptick. Gains in accommodation (+17k), recreation (+19k), and transport (+19k) fit pre-tournament hiring ahead of Canada's June matches.

Rebound, not full recovery. Unemployment at 6.6% remains above the 2017-19 average of 6.0%.
