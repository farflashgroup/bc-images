How much do we spend on Canada Day celebrations?

The federal program behind July 1 events fell from a pandemic spike to about $14M in 2024-25. This year, spending climbs back to $21.2M.

About $5M per year of the 2026-27 rebound is new tax money for fireworks, concerts, and flag-waving nationwide. The program also funds Saint-Jean-Baptiste Day, Multiculturalism Day, and other commemorations. This year, the Canada Day earmark works out to about 16¢ per taxpayer (~31.5M federal tax filers).
