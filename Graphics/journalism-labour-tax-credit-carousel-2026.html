Canadian journalism labour tax credit

Broadcast newsrooms: same labour tax credit?

35%

Labour tax credit

Refundable to the employer on eligible payroll.

35% through Dec. 31, 2026, then 25%

Eligible salary per newsroom employee is capped at $85,000 per tax year, prorated in short years (CRA)

Currently

Licensed broadcasters under the Broadcasting Act sit outside QCJO rules, so TV and radio news largely never claimed.

Cost

Up to 35¢ per qualifying dollar of salary, with a $85,000 ceiling per eligible newsroom employee, prorated in short years.

Consult

Finance Canada is consulting on extending the same credit to audio and audiovisual news production. That mainly means broadcast TV and radio newsrooms left out of QCJO, and could extend to other employer-run news audio and video once draft rules set who qualifies and for which tax years an expansion would apply.

Scale

CRA pegged rough past program cost near $71 million in a written answer, House Question Q-942.
