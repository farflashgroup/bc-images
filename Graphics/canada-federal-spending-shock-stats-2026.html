The accumulated deficit reached $1,266.5 billion at March 31, 2025. That is 41.2% of GDP, with liabilities of $2.2 trillion against $916 billion in assets.

Public Accounts 2025 · Transfer payments

Two-thirds of federal spending is transfer payments

Ottawa sent $359.4 billion to individuals, provinces, territories, and other recipients. That is 66% of all federal expenses in 2024-25.
