Insolvency volumes in Canada hit highest levels since 2009

Equifax reports insolvency volumes rose 18.8% year-over-year in Q1 2026, with homeowner filings up more than 11% from Q4 2025.

37,121

Consumer insolvency filings in Q1 2026, the highest quarterly total since Q1 2009 (38,715), per OSB data cited by Equifax.

Equifax flagged Q1 2026 as a 17-year high for insolvency volumes, driven by strain on mortgage holders renewing at higher rates. Average non-mortgage debt in filings reached $43,300, up from $40,200 two years earlier.
