AI data centres

Purpose

Myth

Most of the usage of these data centres will be to power the creation of AI slop.

Reality

86% non-AI

Most power still serves cloud apps, email, storage, and business IT. AI is the fastest-growing slice, not the whole stack.

Typical hyperscale campus, power by workload, 2024, approximate

54% Cloud
32% IT
14% AI

  • Cloud

    Streaming, SaaS, and web apps people already rely on every day.

  • IT

    Email, databases, backups, and back-office enterprise software.

  • AI

    Coding tools, search, and research workloads. Fastest-growing slice.

Source: Goldman Sachs Research, AI to drive 165% increase in data center power demand by 2030 (2024).
